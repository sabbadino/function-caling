session title: NVIDIA AI Enterprise Registry for AzureML deployments of AI Workflows
abstract: Registries in Azure Machine Learning are repositories of AI assets that are used to define complete MLOPs workflows in production. The NVIDIA AI Enterprise is a set of GPU-optimized libraries for AI applications. We will present ready-to-deploy examples of NVIDIA AI Enterprise end-to-end applications in the form of Azure Machine Learning Resources hosted in the NVIDIA AI Enterprise AzureML Registry for Applications such as Computer Vision, Health, Model Inference Optimization, and Finance.
